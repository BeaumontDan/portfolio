GARDEN OF LIFE

Organic Performance

America's number one nutrition and supplements brand, Garden of Life, wanted to launch the 'cleanest performance line ever'.

This range needed to build on their already certified credentials and brand positioning ‐ Empowering Extraordinary Health ‐ to appeal to and inspire a large athletic and active lifestyle from teen athletes, weekend warriors and mums on the go to elite athletes.

GARDEN OF LIFE

Gummie Goodness

One of my more unusual opportunities ‐ the chance to work with Alicia Silverstone (and Garden of Life) to give her range of MyKind Organic multivitamins a fresh and vibrant new look.

"Every day I try to get all of my essential nutrition from organic, farm-fresh food. But when it's not possible I fill in the gaps with mykind Organics multivitamin gummies. Made from fresh-picked, whole organic fruit and organic, Non-GMO vitamins without using any added sugars or juices, artificial sweeteners, colors, flavors and dyes, they are my next best choice. I hope you enjoy these yummy vitamins as much as I do!"

Alicia Silverstone
