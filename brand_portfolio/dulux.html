DULUX 360

Servicing Growth

Reposition and development of a new identity to replace AkzoNobel's Contract Direct service ‐ Dulux Decorator Center Services.

The task was to simplify and bring in line the existing proposition and identity with other Dulux services and Brands; and make it marketable and compelling to new and existing Contract Direct customers so that they would not want to switch their business to a competitive brand from Contract Direct upon its ending.

The branding needed to be consistently represented through online and offline communications, including interactive PDFs, welcome boxes (and merchandise), printed information sheets and leaflets.
