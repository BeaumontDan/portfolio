ALPEN

Brighter Spoonfuls

New competition with a fresh approach to the muesli category had left Alpen looking like the dry healthy option. Each pack captures a scene focusing on the goodness of life outdoors in what was previously a static rugged mountain range. The spoon icon heroes the balance of taste and the natural positivity of starting the morning right. A new, clean and modern Alpen brandmark completed the brands rejuvenated place in the category.
