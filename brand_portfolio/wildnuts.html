WILD NUTS

Feed Your Animal Instincts

    • Pentawards Nomination
    • 2019

A new brand identity to bring to life a unique product proposition, engage consumers and differentiate the broader competition at the time. My wild animal illustrations emanate from and evolve out of the product photography; chosen to capture each exotic product delivery.

I developed each product name to add even further intrigue and emphasis to the exotic sources of these fruity, nutty blends and create a brand with real personality and provoke reactions with a packaging design that would grab attention on any shelf fixture.

I wrote the copy for each of the back of pack, marketing descriptions, to capture the exploratory nature of the flavour combinations and inspiring provenance.
