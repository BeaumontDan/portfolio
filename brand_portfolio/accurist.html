ACCURIST LONDON

Essential Wrist Attire

Accurist was under performing in 2013 and in very real danger of extinction. Time Products, seen (through their brands) as the makers of traditional timepieces, took the opportunity to acquire the brand and immediately set the task to reposition and revive Accurist as a stylish, contemporary British brand.

'Essential Wrist Attire' was created as a new brand essence to bring contemporary fashion appeal to the brand whilst retaining its British history and real watch making expertise. The first results from the buyers within the industry were been very positive and the brand saw a positive lift throughout the festive season of 2014, ensuring the brands survival.

The Essential TV Campaign

The first Accurist TV campaign since the rebrand, for which I was involved in the storyboarding and art direction of the film shoot.

The creative concept was 'aspiration by association' and dialling up the 'Essential Wrist Attire' position by placing Accurist alongside items of luxury.

Storyline: An aspirational romance, a story laced with luxury.We follow two people as they part-take in a journey to the heart of time in London, their Accurist watches ever a part of their special moments.
