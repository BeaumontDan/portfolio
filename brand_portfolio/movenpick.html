MÖVENPICK ICE CREAM

A Spoonful of Extraordinary

Mövenpick, the 'Super-premium' Swiss Ice Cream brand, needed to tell their story in more forward and engaging way across all brand touch-points including their packaging as they sought to expand their already global presence into new markets, including the UK.

Whilst unmistakably Swiss, the branding at the time (below) was failing to communicate any of the brands personality, emotive or sensory engagement. They were losing out to Ben & Jerry's with their 'bonkers Ice Cream makers' personality and a rejuvenated, less exclusive Häagen-Dazs.

Their single believe that “the best moments in life are simple things done in extraordinary ways" became the brand essence - a spoonful of extraordinary - which encapsulates belonging to the moment; simple moments of indulging yourself and the pure joy of making the simple extraordinary... one spoon at a time.
