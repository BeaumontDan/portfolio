YOUNG'S GASTRO

Seafood Gastronomy

Sub-brand creation for Youngapos's Seafood, launching a premium range of seafood favourites. Gastro was designed to suggest and appeal to 'everyday special occasions' ‐ date nights dinner for two. Positioning the range as restaurant quality meals sought to establish Young's as expert seafood chefs above their well established fishermen credentials.
